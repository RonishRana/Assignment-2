The Avengers

Avengers Assemble

  • Hero

    Iron Man

    Tony Stark, a genius inventor and billionaire, becomes Iron Man with his advanced suit of armor. A key member of the Avengers, he evolves from a selfish industrialist to a hero committed to protecting the world. Iron Man symbolizes ingenuity, bravery, and redemption.

  • Hero

    Dr. Strange

    Dr. Stephen Strange transforms from an arrogant surgeon into the Sorcerer Supreme after a career-ending accident. He masters mystic arts to protect Earth from magical threats, embodying a journey of redemption and the merging of magic with reality.

  • Hero

    Groot

    Groot, a sentient, tree-like creature, says only "I am Groot" but conveys deep loyalty and bravery. A Guardian of the Galaxy, he's known for his regenerative ability and strong bond with Rocket Raccoon. Groot embodies innocence, sacrifice, and the importance of actions over words in his journey from enigma to hero.
